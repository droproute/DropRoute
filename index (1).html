Who We Are

Travel built for
the music-obsessed.

Drop Route was born out of a simple truth: the best travel stories always happen around music. Not just at the festival — on the way there, in the Airbnb at 4am, in the taxi with strangers who became your crew.

We're not a travel agency. We're music lovers who figured out how to make the journey as good as the drop. We curate end-to-end rave travel — from flights and stays to festival passes, pre-parties, and secret after-hours.

Whether you want to hit Sunburn in Goa, Wonderfruit in Thailand, or find underground techno in Berlin — we build your trip around the sound.
