About Drop Route

Travel the world.
Dance to the culture.

Drop Route is a travel curation platform for music lovers — we build travel plans that go beyond the festival gate, deep into the culture, food, and soul of every destination.

Every itinerary we craft blends the best local experiences — hidden neighbourhoods, street food trails, cultural landmarks — with the world's most electric music events and parties.

Whether it's a three-day Goa beach rave with sunrise yoga, a Delhi underground night followed by Old City exploration, or a Bangkok festival trip with temple runs — we map the whole journey, not just the drop.
